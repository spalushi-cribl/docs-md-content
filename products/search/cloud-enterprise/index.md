# Enterprise Cloud


Explore the benefits of the Cribl.Cloud Enterprise Plan.

---

With a Cribl.Cloud Enterprise plan, you gain additional flexibility and options. These options include configuring and managing multiple [Stream Worker Groups](/stream/worker-groups) or
[Edge Fleets](/edge/fleet-management), integrating external SSO authentication, and role-based access control on resources.


> For other Enterprise features, see [Pricing](https://cribl.io/pricing/).
>
{.box .info}

Cribl.Cloud Enterprise also adds:

- Full control of [Member Permissions](cloud-managing#member-roles) on your Cribl.Cloud Organization.
- The hybrid deployment option.
- The Leader resides in Cribl.Cloud, with access to diverse Worker deployments. Cribl manages the Leader's availability.
