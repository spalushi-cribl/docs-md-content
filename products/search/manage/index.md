# Manage Cribl Search and Cribl.Cloud


Manage Cribl Search, your Cribl.Cloud Organization, and its Members, Permissions, usage, and billing.

---

Your Cribl Search instance is a part of a Cribl.Cloud Organization. This section covers how to manage the Organization itself, your Plan (credits and billing), and several aspects of access management and resource sharing.

## Management Options by Permission {#who}

If you're an Organization Owner or Organization Admin, you can manage various aspects of your Cribl.Cloud Organization. You also automatically inherit all the Permissions of a Search Admin.

If you're a Search Admin, you can control most aspects of your Cribl Search environment.

For an overview of who can do what, see:

- [Organization Member Permissions](cloud-managing#member-roles)
- [Search Member Permissions](cloud-managing#search-member-roles)

## Workspaces

[Workspaces](workspaces) are isolated environments within your Cribl.Cloud Organization, each with its own Leader and
access control. To make the most of them, see the following sections:

- [Configure Workspaces](workspaces-configuring): Add, edit, or delete your Cribl.Cloud Workspaces.
- [Members and Teams](members-and-teams): Fine-tune access control within your Workspaces, by configuring Members and
  Teams.
