# Cribl.Cloud Access and Usage


Get an overview of the Cribl.Cloud environment that hosts Cribl Search and complementary Cribl products.

---

Cribl.Cloud provides a dedicated environment that hosts [Cribl Search](/search/), [Cribl Stream](/stream/), [Cribl Edge](/edge/), and [Cribl Lake](/lake/), enabling you to start using Cribl products immediately. Cribl manages and scales infrastructure on your behalf.


## Cribl.Cloud Resources {#resources}

See these topics that cover creating and administering your Cribl.Cloud account, Organization, and authorization:

- [Quick Setup](getting-started-guide): Register a Cribl.Cloud Organization to get started with Cribl Search.
- [Manage Cribl.Cloud](cloud-portal): Learn to switch between Cribl.Cloud Organizations and configure your Organization details.
- [Manage Cribl.Cloud Members](cloud-managing): Manage Cribl.Cloud access control – Members, invites, and Permissions.
- [FinOps Center](cloud-billing): Track your credit usage and billing across all Cribl products.

## Cribl.Cloud Enterprise Plan {#enterprise}

With a Cribl.Cloud Enterprise plan, you gain additional flexibility and options. These options include: 

- Full role-based access control over [Member Permissions](cloud-managing#member-roles) and resources. 

- Integrating external SSO authentication. 

- Configuring and managing multiple [Stream Worker Groups](/stream/worker-groups) and [Edge Fleets](/edge/fleet-management).

- [Hybrid](/stream/cloud-enterprise#hybrid) Stream Worker Groups.


> For other Enterprise features, see [Pricing](https://cribl.io/pricing/).
