# Tutorials


Follow these tutorials to get a one-stop overview of the interface
and data flow functionalities of Cribl Stream.

- [Cribl Stream Tour](tour) covers QuickConnect and Data Routing, focusing on Cribl.Cloud.
- [Get Started with Cribl Stream](getting-started-guide) presents a more complex walkthrough of configuring a data flow, based on an on-prem deployment.
- [Distributed Quick Start](distributed-guide) guides you through setting up a Distributed Deployment of Cribl Stream.
