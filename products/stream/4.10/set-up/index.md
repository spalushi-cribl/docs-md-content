# Setting Up Cribl Stream


Cribl Stream offers different types of deployments that you can choose based on your needs:

- [Cribl.Cloud](deploy-cloud), including a [hybrid](cloud-enterprise#hybrid) option
- [On-prem deployment](deploy-types), including:
  - [Single-instance deployment](deploy-single-instance)
  - [Distributed deployment](deploy-distributed)
  - [Orchestrated deployment](orchestrated-deployment)
  - [Docker deployment](deploy-docker)

To prepare for a deployment and gather decision-making information,
consult [Deployment Planning](deploy-planning).
