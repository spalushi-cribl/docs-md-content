# Data Sampling


Data sampling lets you analyze and troubleshoot data and plan transformations you need to perform
on a selected, representative sample of real-life data.

The following usecases guide you through preparing and using data samples in specific situations:

- [Access Logs: Apache, ELB, CDN, S3, Etc.](usecase-access-logs)
