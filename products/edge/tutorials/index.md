# Tutorials


Follow these tutorials to get a one-stop overview of the interface
and data flow functionalities of Cribl Edge.

- [Getting Started Tutorial](getting-started-tutorial) walks you through basic Cribl Edge functionalities,
  showing you how to capture sample data and send it to Cribl Stream.
- [Configuring Cribl Edge](usecase-configuring-edge) offers practical guidance on configuring your new deployment to efficiently collect, process, and forward your Cribl Edge data.
- [Forward Custom Data with Cribl Edge Tags](usecase-custom-tags) shows how to add unique identifiers or metadata to each event for better control of data at scale.
- [Monitoring your Infrastructure with Cribl Edge](usecase-infrastructure-monitoring) presents how to monitor infrastructure by collecting, processing, and forwarding data with low overhead cost and resources.
- [Monitor and Troubleshoot Cribl Edge](usecase-monitoring-troubleshooting) explores key metrics to monitor for optimal Cribl Edge operation, discusses how to gather this information, and provides effective monitoring strategies and general troubleshooting guidelines.
