# Federal User Onboarding Guide


> The following draft provides early access to the Cribl.Cloud Government product release. Features or functionality described are not considered binding commitments and are subject to change at the discretion of Cribl at any time for any reason without notice. This information should not be relied upon in making purchasing decisions.
{.box .info}

This high-level guide outlines the key steps for you to access and use Cribl.Cloud Government.

1. **Complete the customer onboarding process**: To begin using Cribl.Cloud Government, contact your designated account representative for onboarding. 
2. **Work with Cribl to establish a dedicated Cribl.Cloud Government environment**: Your account representative will guide you through the steps to establish your dedicated environment.
3. **Implement required security controls**: For details, see [Secure Your Cribl.Cloud Government Deployment](securing-cloud-gov).
4. **Get data flowing by configuring Sources and Destinations**: For details, see [Explore Cribl Products](fed_products_tutorials).

## Supported Regions

Cribl.Cloud Government is available within AWS US East/West Regions. Worker Groups within the Cribl.Cloud Government environment cannot be created on Microsoft Azure.
 
| Region Name | Region Code    |
| :---------- | :------------- |
| US East (N. Virginia) | `us-east-1` |
| US East (Ohio)        | `us-east-2` |
| US West (Oregon)      | `us-west-2` |

### Getting Started Guides {#guides}

The following guides will help you with the initial setup and management of your Cribl.Cloud Government organization. Cribl.Cloud Government offers comparable features to our commercial platform, and these guides are applicable to both environments. 

| Tutorial                                                    | Description                                                                                                                               |
|----------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [Register Cribl.Cloud Organization](/stream/cloud-initial-setup/) | Register your Cribl.Cloud Government organization.                                                                                |
| [Manage Cribl.Cloud Organization](/stream/cloud-portal/)      | Manage your organization settings, including network configuration, user access control, and billing information.              |
| [Cribl.Cloud Enterprise](/stream/cloud-enterprise/)          | Get an overview of Cribl.Cloud Enterprise, which is conceptually comparable to the Cribl.Cloud Government enterprise offering.              |
| [Manage Cribl.Cloud Worker Groups](/stream/cloud-workers/)    | Create a Cribl.Cloud Worker Group. The steps for Cribl.Cloud Government are similar, but with a more limited scope: Azure is not supported, and fewer regions are available for deployment even in supported clouds like AWS. |
